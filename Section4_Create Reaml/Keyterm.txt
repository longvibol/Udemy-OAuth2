It is a space that allows us to manage a set of users, their roles and their credentials.

So when the user logs in, they log in into this space, they log in, into this realm and users in

one realm, they are isolated from users, from another realm on the server.

- A tenant = a Realm in Keycloak.

A Realm is an isolated space where you manage:

Users

Roles

Groups

Clients (applications)

Authentication settings

Each realm is completely separated from others.
